Rock, Paper, Scissors

Game Design mid semester project, This was my first ever game.

Gameplay: You can play against the computer in a game of rock, paper, scissors with cool animations.

Features: 2 unique characters with different behaviours and a secret character the player can unlock, A main menu and a character select screen, Unique animations for all outcomes of a match, Animations for all characters for every game state.

Technical Information: Made in Visual studio with C++ and the SFML library, all art was made in Aseprite

Shooting Game

Introduction to Object Oriented Programming end of year project.

Gameplay: You play as a guy with a gun in an arena where monsters keep coming at you endlessly, as you kill more monsters the eye in the center of the screen opens more and more and eventually doubles the difficulty.

Features: A character the player can move and fire a gun, Two enemies with unique behaviour that the player can kill, A kill tracker which caluclates a score, A main menu which lets you start the game and see your statistics, a dynamic hud that displays your health.

Technical Information: The game was made in Visual studio using C++ and SFML, the sprites were made in Aseprite and the menu art was made in photoshop.

Journey through Abyss

Game Design end of year group project (3 people).

Gameplay: You play as a knight wandering through an abyss who must fight against monsters walking and flying around the abyss. You can switch between your sword and gun to defeat them.

Features: A main menu, character that can move, jump and attack with two weapons, the ability to switch equipped weapons, A pause screen with a map showing location and equipped weapon, a dynamic hud that shows health and equipped weapon, 2 enemies with different behaviour.

Technical Information: Made in C++ with the SFML library, sprites were made in Aseprite and the higher resolution art was made in photoshop.

Dragon Game

User Interface Programming end of semester project.

Gameplay: You play as a snail who has to defeat a dragon, You have the ability to travel between the present and past at will and you can find abilities that will help you in fighting the dragon.

Features:

  • A menu that lets you save and load data
  • A character who can move and attack
  • bomb upgrade that can be found and used to destroy walls
  • fully functioning dragon boss battle that reacts to the users actions
  • The ability to switch between the past and present at will which will be different and have different interactions and dialogue
  • A dialogue text box system which allows for cutscenes and interaction with objects and an ending where you must go back to the past to stop the dragon


Technical Information: Menus created with HTML5. Game created in Javascript with the JS Canvas.
